ABOUT ME


As a user experience designer I am always looking to tackle old problems with fresh approaches. I am energized by creating delightful experiences that prioritize the motivations, needs and desires of a products users. 

My insane curiosity propelled me to serve internationally in places like Senegal, Haiti and Guatemala. My time working with orphaned children inspired my better half of 15+ years and I to adopt three children.  These adoptions thrust us into the world of Autism, Type-1 Diabetes, ADHD, Selective Mutism and premature births. As a father and natural problem solver I am always looking for better ways to care for my kids’ needs. Most recently, my family raised $25,000 to purchase a diabetic alert dog for my son, Andy - he also sports a sexy Continual Glucose Monitoring device from Dexcom, which is a technological marvel.

I aim to leverage my visonary DNA by balancing ideation with execution. This holds true for whether I am shaping wood, forming sentences or creating memorable experiences for my family. My free time is absorbed by quiet, books, walks, biking and coaching.
